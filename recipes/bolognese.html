Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish with a rich meat sauce made with ground beef, tomatoes, and vegetables, traditionally served over spaghetti.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef: Lean ground beef for a hearty and flavorful sauce.

  • Onion and garlic: Adds depth and richness to the sauce.

  • Tomato products: Use crushed tomatoes and tomato paste for a rich tomato base.

  • Carrots and celery: Classic vegetables to add sweetness and texture.

  • Spices: Oregano, thyme, and bay leaves enhance the flavors.

  • Red wine: Optional but adds a deep flavor to the sauce.

  • Spaghetti: Cooked al dente for serving with the sauce.

  • Parmesan cheese: For sprinkling on top before serving.

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery, and saut√© until soft.
  2. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and red wine (if using). Add herbs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
