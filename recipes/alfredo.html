Chicken Alfredo

Description:

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy pasta dish made with fettuccine noodles, a rich Alfredo sauce, and tender chicken breasts.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts for a hearty protein component.

  • Heavy cream and butter: The base of the Alfredo sauce.

  • Parmesan cheese: Grated Parmesan adds flavor to the sauce.

  • Garlic: Minced garlic adds a rich flavor.

  • Fettuccine noodles: Traditional pasta for Alfredo.

  • Parsley: Fresh parsley for garnish.

Steps:

  1. Cook the fettuccine noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Cook chicken breasts until golden brown and cooked through, then set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, melt butter and add garlic. Saut√© until fragrant, then add heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  4. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens.
  5. Slice the cooked chicken and toss it with the pasta in the Alfredo sauce.
  6. Garnish with parsley and more Parmesan cheese before serving.
